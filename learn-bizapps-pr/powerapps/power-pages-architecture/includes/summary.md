Provisioning a Power Pages site is a straightforward process, but it adds numerous features and components to a Microsoft Dataverse environment.

In this module, you learned about:

- The fact that a full, working  Power Pages site is provisioned from a starter template and is configured for specific business requirements as opposed to creating a new application.
- Various components of the Power Pages architecture such as portal solutions, portal metadata, and the portal web application.
- Site provisioning steps, including where and when specific components are added to a Microsoft Power Platform or Dynamics 365 environment.
- Various  Power Pages maker and admin tools that are used to manage content and portal features.
