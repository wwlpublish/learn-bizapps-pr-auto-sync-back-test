## Task - Explore the Manage Issues app

1. Select the **Manage Issues** tab on your screen.

    You may need to select **more** if it's not appearing.

    > [!div class="mx-imgBorder"]
    > [![Screenshot of Microsoft Teams with the Manage Issues button highlighted.](../media/manage-issues.png)](../media/manage-issues.png#lightbox)

1. Within the Manage issues app, select the **Issue templates** tab.

    > [!div class="mx-imgBorder"]
    > [![Screenshot of exploring the Manage Issues App on the Issue templates tab.](../media/issue-templates.png)](../media/issue-templates.png#lightbox)

1. Select **Add category**.

    > [!div class="mx-imgBorder"]
    > [![Screenshot of adding new category to Manage Issues app.](../media/add-category.png)](../media/add-category.png#lightbox)

1. Enter **Kitchen** as the category title, then select **Update icon**. Select the Food icon. Then, select **Save** to create the new category.

    > [!div class="mx-imgBorder"]
    > [![Screenshot of adding new category to Manage Issues app with Update icon highlighted.](../media/update-icon.png)](../media/update-icon.png#lightbox)

1. With the Kitchen category selected, select **Add issue template**.

    > [!div class="mx-imgBorder"]
    > [![Screenshot of adding new category to Manage Issues app with add issue template highlighted.](../media/add-issue.png)](../media/add-issue.png#lightbox)

1. Enter the following information into the template:

    **Title**: Hot Water Not Working

    **Due within**: 2

    **Auto assign issues to**: *your account*

    > [!div class="mx-imgBorder"]
    > [![Screenshot of adding new category to Manage Issues app with title, due within, and auto assign issues to values filled in.](../media/hot-water.png)](../media/hot-water.png#lightbox)

1. Select **Add question**. For Question 1, type in **How long has this issue existed?**

    > [!div class="mx-imgBorder"]
    > [![Screenshot of adding new category to Manage Issues app with the add question button highlighted.](../media/add-question.png)](../media/add-question.png#lightbox)

1. Select **Add question** again, and type in Question 2 as **Has this happened before?**

    > [!div class="mx-imgBorder"]
    > [![Screenshot of adding new category to Manage Issues app with a second question added.](../media/questions.png)](../media/questions.png#lightbox)

1. In the **Supporting information** field, type in **A plumber will be called within the next day to fix the issue**.

    > [!div class="mx-imgBorder"]
    > [![Screenshot of adding new category to Manage Issues app with supporting information added.](../media/supporting.png)](../media/supporting.png#lightbox)

1. Select **Save** to store your template.

    > [!div class="mx-imgBorder"]
    > [![Screenshot of adding new category to Manage Issues app with the Save button highlighted.](../media/save-template.png)](../media/save-template.png#lightbox)

## Task - Explore the Issue reporting app

1. Select the **Issue reporting** tab of your team.

    > [!div class="mx-imgBorder"]
    > [![Screenshot of the Issue Reporting tab in Microsoft Teams.](../media/issue-reporting-tab.png)](../media/issue-reporting-tab.png#lightbox)

1. Select **Allow**.

    > [!div class="mx-imgBorder"]
    > [![Screenshot of the Issue Reporting app almost there dialog with the allow button highlighted.](../media/allow-issue.png)](../media/allow-issue.png#lightbox)

1. Select **Report an issue**.

    > [!div class="mx-imgBorder"]
    > [![Screenshot of Issue Reporting app - Report an issue.](../media/report.png)](../media/report.png#lightbox)

1. Select **Select an issue type** and select **Kitchen**. Select the **Hot Water Not Working** template.

    > [!div class="mx-imgBorder"]
    > [![Screenshot of Issue Reporting app - select Kitchen category.](../media/kitchen.png)](../media/kitchen.png#lightbox)

1. In the **Location** field, type in **Dallas Store**.

    > [!NOTE]
    > You can turn on the GPS switch to record the coordinates of your location.

1. Enter a Description, for example, **No hot water is coming out of the tap from the sink in the main kitchen**.

1. For the question **How long has this issue existed?**, type in **3 days**.

1. For the question **Has this happened before?**, type in **No**.

1. Select **Submit issue**.

    > [!div class="mx-imgBorder"]
    > [![Screenshot of Issue Reporting app - submit issue.](../media/submit-issue.png)](../media/submit-issue.png#lightbox)

1. Select **View in Tasks** to launch Tasks in a new tab, and you can see the issue you reported.

    > [!div class="mx-imgBorder"]
    > ![Screenshot of Issue Reporting app - view in tasks.](../media/view.png)

    > [!div class="mx-imgBorder"]
    > [![Screenshot of Issue Reporting app - detailed view in tasks.](../media/view-tasks.png)](../media/view-tasks.png#lightbox)

1. From Tasks, you can manage the details and status of the task, such as change the Progress, Priority, Due Date and add attachments. For now, just change the **Progress** to **In progress**.

    > [!div class="mx-imgBorder"]
    > [![Screenshot of reporting issue- change to in progress.](../media/progress.png)](../media/progress.png#lightbox)

1. Go back to the tab in your browser with Microsoft Teams open. Select **Return to home**. You'll now see a summary of the issues your user has reported, where a summary of each can be viewed.

    > [!div class="mx-imgBorder"]
    > ![Screenshot of Issue reporting app - click Return to home.](../media/return.png)

    > [!div class="mx-imgBorder"]
    > [![Screenshot of reporting issue.](../media/report-issue.png)](../media/report-issue.png#lightbox)
